🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Care Connect SL ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, address
  • Health Information: Medical history, conditions, referral details
  • Account Credentials: Username, password, role

Usage Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, referral history

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Process referrals, connect patients with providers
  • Communication: Send referral updates, appointment reminders
  • Improvement: Enhance our platform and develop new features
  • Compliance: Meet legal obligations and protect rights

4. Information Sharing

We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you authorize us to share information
  • With Healthcare Providers: When you submit a referral
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Access Control: Strict role-based access to health information
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data

Contact us at privacy@careconnect.sl.
