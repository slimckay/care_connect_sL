2. Our Services

Care Connect SL provides a platform for:

  • Submitting and managing healthcare referrals
  • Connecting patients with healthcare providers
  • Coordinating home visits and clinic appointments

Medical Disclaimer: We are a referral platform and do not provide direct medical care.

15. Commission and Fees

Care Connect SL charges a commission on successful referrals to sustain the platform.

15.1 Commission Rates

  • Doctors: 15% of the consultation fee
  • Clinics and Hospitals: 20% of the consultation fee

15.2 How Commissions Work

  • Commissions are deducted automatically when a patient pays for a referral
  • The provider receives the remaining amount (85% or 80%)
  • Patients are not charged any additional fees beyond the consultation fee
  • All transactions are recorded in your transaction history

💡 100% Free for Patients

Patients never pay any platform fees—only the consultation fee.
