🚀 Getting Started

Care Connect SL is a healthcare referral platform connecting patients, caregivers, and healthcare providers across Sierra Leone. We make it easy to submit referrals, request home visits, and coordinate care.

Click the "Register" button in the top right corner. Fill in your name, email, password, and select your role. After registration, you'll receive a confirmation email.

📋 Referrals

Go to the "Referrals" page and fill out the referral form. Include the patient's name, contact information, location, and a brief description of their condition.

Submitting a referral through Care Connect SL is free. However, healthcare services provided by clinics may have associated costs.

💰 Commission & Fees

We charge a commission on successful referrals: 15% for doctors and 20% for clinics/hospitals. Patients pay no platform fees.
