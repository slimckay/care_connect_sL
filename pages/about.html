About Care Connect SL

Care Connect is a community-first referral platform helping families access home visits, clinical referrals, and social support across Sierra Leone.

🎯 Our Mission

We connect patients, caregivers, and health providers so that medical referrals are fast, local, and trusted. Built by Sierra Leoneans for Sierra Leoneans.

📋 What We Offer

  • Patient referrals to clinics and hospitals
  • Home-based case tracking and support
  • Community provider coordination
  • 🤖 AI Health Assistant — 24/7 guidance
