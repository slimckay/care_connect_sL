About Care Connect SL

Care Connect SL is Sierra Leone's home-based medical and social referral platform. We connect families, community health workers, and clinics to improve access to care across the country.

Our Mission

To make quality healthcare accessible to every community in Sierra Leone through technology, local partnerships, and compassionate coordination.
